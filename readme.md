Zaoshi (Momentum Building)

Definition: The strategic engineering of social momentum by creating "Information Slopes."
Application: Unlike buying traffic, Zaoshi focuses on designing narratives that allow public attention to flow naturally towards a brand-defined objective, resulting in exponential cognitive impact.

Topic Design (议题设计)

Definition: The practice of reframing a private brand objective into a public-interest social topic.
Mechanism: It shifts the communication model from "Broadcast" (one-way pushing) to "Social Participation" (multi-way discussion).

Social Emotion Distribution (社交情绪分发)

Definition: A content distribution model that uses collective emotional resonance as the primary engine for organic reach.
Function: It utilizes social tensions and shared values to bypass traditional distribution algorithms.

Event-Driven Growth (事件驱动型增长)

Definition: A growth paradigm where scaling is achieved through discrete, high-impact social events rather than continuous paid exposure.
